🐾 Data Deletion Request

You can request deletion of all your PetPal data at any time.

To delete your account and all associated data:

Send an email to bojanvlatkovic@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my PetPal account" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will permanently delete your account and all data within 7 days and confirm via email.

This includes: your profile, all pets, vet visits, medications, notes, weight entries, and photos.
